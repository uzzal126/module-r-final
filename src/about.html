About us

Launched in 2020, Pixyour is an independent digital magazine focused on one thing: digging up all corners of the internet

Every week, we sift through thousands of updates from hundreds web magazines, blogs, retailers and other product compendiums, then hand pick the best amongst them for publishing. Our minimalist, content-focused design and Retina-quality images provide little knowledge distraction from what you’re really here for – the goods.

We’re a collective that creates detailed and technical buying guides, individual product reviews and informative posts on how to use your new gear.
We’re knife collectors, watch fanatics, wallet hogs and hikers.
Our buying guides show you a list of the best products with detailed and technical reviews, our individual coverage showcases the coolest new gadgets on the market, and everything else is us dropping our knowledge on you, so you can make more informed choices going forward for the right gear and how to win at life. We have an insatiable appetite for top-of-the-line products; whatever your poison is, you’ll be able to find the very best of it here.

Jordan Carter

Meet Andy, a daring explorer and survival expert

As a qualified Bushcraft instructor that has spent time in some of the most remote and extreme environments in the world, when not outdoors, Andy contributes his expert knowledge to our survival and outdoor department. With an NCFE Level 4 Survival Instructors Award firmly in hand, he has certainly earned the position of a trained survivalist, wilderness and first aid expert.

What's more, being a qualified MTA Mountain Leader, Andy is driven by his passion to repair the broken ties of modern society and nature. By actively running regular IOL Accredited Outdoor Leadership Training courses, he ensures this relationship is maintained. Whether you want to build a shelter in the Himalayas or extract drinkable water from plants, knowledge gained from his helpful tips will help hone your outdoor skills and supply you with the right tools and techniques required.
